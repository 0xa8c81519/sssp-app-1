Description
bStable is an open-source, automated market maker (AMM) mechanism especially designed to enable token swaps between any stablecoin on Binance Smart Chain (BSC) at low fees and with minimal price slippage. Combining an efficient, decentralized stablecoin swap platform on BSC with a multi-stablecoin “savings account,” bStable will allow users to add their funds to the protocol’s liquidity to earn passive income. Soon, getting from stablecoin to stablecoin on BSC will become as easy as possible.
